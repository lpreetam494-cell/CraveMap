# CraveMap: Sovereign Food Intelligence
**Hackathon Pitch Deck Script (10 Slides)**

---

## Slide 1: Title & Vision
**Visual:** Sovereign Logo + Cyan Glow.
**Script:** "Good evening judges. We save hundreds of food reels, we save Instagram posts, we talk about food in chat—and yet, every Friday night, we still ask the same question: 'What should we eat?' We are CraveMap, and we’re building the world’s first Sovereign Food Intelligence Agent."

## Slide 2: The Problem (Digital Hoarding)
**Visual:** A messy collage of Instagram Reels, Yelp links, and WhatsApp messages.
**Script:** "Users suffer from 'Digital Food Hoarding.' 50% of saved restaurants are never visited because they are lost in the noise. Current apps (Yelp/Google) are search engines, not agents. They don't know your history, your cravings, or your friends' dietary restrictions."

## Slide 3: The Solution (CraveMap Agent)
**Visual:** Image of the 'Sovereign Home' dashboard with the 94% Match.
**Script:** "CraveMap is an autonomous agent system built on OpenClaw. It doesn't wait for you to search. it watches your digital inputs, learns your 'Craving Cycles,' and proactively suggests the right meal at the right time. It is your Sovereign Food Brain."

## Slide 4: OpenClaw Architecture (The Engine)
**Visual:** Diagram showing Social Hunter -> Taste Alchemist -> Lifestyle Operator.
**Script:** "Our system is powered by three specialized OpenClaw Agents:
1. **Social Hunter:** Extracts metadata from raw social links and chats.
2. **Taste Alchemist:** Uses local memory to synthesize preference and craving cycles.
3. **Lifestyle Operator:** Executes actions—sending notifications and drafting group polls."

## Slide 5: Demo (The Magic Moment)
**Visual:** Video or Live Demo of the 'AI Feed' updating in real-time.
**Script:** "Let's look at the OpenClaw Operations feed. As a user saves a Reel, you see the Social Hunter extracting 'Japanese Ramen' in real-time. Moments later, the Lifestyle Operator detects rain in San Francisco and proactively generates a 'Rainy Day Ramen' alert. This isn't a search; it's ambient intelligence."

## Slide 6: Preference Topology (Group Consensus)
**Visual:** The Triangle Topology UI (Image 4).
**Script:** "Groups are hard. Marcus wants spicy, Sarah is vegan, and the budget is tight. Our Consensus Engine calculates the 'Preference Topology'—finding the mathematical 'Sweet Spot' between Cuisine, Distance, and Budget to suggest a restaurant with 92% Group Harmony."

## Slide 7: Sovereign Privacy (The Vault)
**Visual:** The 'Data Vault' UI (Image 7).
**Script:** "Your food data is intimate. In the 'Sovereign' era, your memory belongs to you. All CraveMap data is stored locally. The user controls who sees their history through our Data Vault, ensuring privacy that centralized delivery apps can never provide."

## Slide 8: Technology Stack
**Visual:** Icons: Node.js, React, Socket.io, Groq, OpenClaw, Telegraf.
**Script:** "We built this using a modern stack: OpenClaw for agent orchestration, Groq for lightning-fast metadata extraction, and WebSockets for real-time agent transparency on the dashboard."

## Slide 9: Future Roadmap
**Visual:** Timeline showing: 1. Telegram/WhatsApp Bot Launch, 2. Google Maps API Integration, 3. Community Recipe Sharing.
**Script:** "Next, we are moving beyond simulation. We are integrating the Telegram Bot API and Google Places to bring CraveMap to every smartphone, becoming the ultimate utility for the social food explorer."

## Slide 10: Conclusion & Q&A
**Visual:** 'Lock It In' Button.
**Script:** "CraveMap isn't just an app; it's a partner in your culinary journey. Don't just save it. Crave it. Lock it in. Thank you."
